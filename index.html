USA

14 days.

Start the tour!

Básic information.

Departure by plane from Votuporanga to São Paulo at 9:00 PM on July 1st. Departure by plane from São Paulo to Orlando, USA at 6:00 AM on July 2nd. Expected arrival in Orlando, USA at 6:00 PM on July 2nd. The duration of the trip is 14 days. The current season at the destination is summer. The expected climate is hot and humid with an average temperature of 28°C.

What to bring?

For a summer trip to the United States, check the airline restrictions and bring travel documents, lightweight and comfortable clothes, swimwear, suitable footwear, accessories like sunglasses and hats, sunscreen, personal hygiene items, electronics with adapters, medications, cash, and cards. Adjust the list according to your specific needs and destination. In case of colder weather upon arrival in the United States, we will bring winter clothing as a precaution, such as sweaters, sweatpants, and hats.

Day 1:

Check-in at Rosen Inn International hotel. Rent a car and explore downtown Orlando. Have dinner at STK Steakhouse. Return to the hotel.

Day 2:

Visit the Disney parks in Orlando and explore the park's attractions, such as the castles and roller coasters, as well as try the themed food. Return to the hotel.

Day 3:

Return to the Disney park and complete the visit to the park's attractions. Later, go to the cinema and watch the main featured movie. Return to the hotel.

Day 4:

Check-out from Rosen Inn International hotel and take a taxi to the city of Miami. Check-in at Best Western On The Bay Inn & Marina hotel. Rest from the previous day.

Day 5:

Visit the beaches of Miami, such as Miami Beach, and have lunch with portions and snacks right on the beach. Return to the hotel.

Day 6:

Enjoy the Miami beaches again and have lunch at Garden House Latin Restaurant. In the late afternoon, catch the 6:00 PM flight to New York.

Day 7:

Arrive at The Central Park North hotel and check-in. Have lunch at Per Se restaurant. Visit Central Park. Have dinner at Boucherie Union Square restaurant. Return to the hotel.

Day 8:

Visit Times Square and explore its center and surroundings. Take a helicopter tour of the Big Apple. Have dinner at Cereto's pizzeria. Return to the hotel.

Day 9:

Rent a car to visit Brooklyn, where the location of "Everybody Hates Chris" was filmed. Have a quick snack and explore downtown New York. Return to the hotel and have dinner there.

Day 10:

Take a flight to Washington D.C. at 10:00 AM. Check-in at DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel and have lunch there. Visit the White House in the USA. Have dinner at Old Ebbitt Grill restaurant.

Day 11:

Have lunch at the hotel. Go to Tanger Outlets Pittsburgh for clothes shopping and visit similar stores. Have dinner at McDonald's.

Day 12:

Check-out from DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel and return to Miami by bus, and head back to the beaches. Stay only one night at Riu Plaza Miami Beach hotel and have fast food for dinner.

Day 13:

Take the flight back to Brazil at 11:00 AM. Expected arrival in São Paulo at 11:00 PM. Check-in at Hotel Millennium.

Day 14:

Conclusion of the trip by returning to Votuporanga on an executive bus and concluding the journey.
